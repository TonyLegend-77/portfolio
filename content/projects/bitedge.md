## Bitedge - AI Backtest Engine

**Live Demo:** [bitedge.vercel.app](https://bitedge.vercel.app)

### Overview
Bitedge is a sophisticated AI backtest engine designed for both human traders and autonomous AI agents. It enables rapid testing of trading strategies with real market data and AI-driven optimizations.

### My Role
**Developer & Founder**

### Key Features
- ⚙️ **Dashboard** - Strategy performance analytics
- 📊 **Advanced Backtesting** - Multi-asset, multi-timeframe testing
- 🤖 **AI Optimization** - ML-powered strategy optimization
- 📈 **Perps Engine** - Futures and perpetuals testing
- 🔄 **Arb Scanner** - Arbitrage opportunity backtesting
- 📡 **Market Pulse** - Historical market analysis
- 🛡️ **Guardian Mode** - Risk management simulation

### Technology Stack

**Frontend**
